Our Adoption Story

How we became a safe haven for pets in need.

  • 2015

    Idea is Born

    The initial idea for a community-focused, no-kill shelter was developed by a small group of passionate volunteers.

  • March 2016

    Doors Open

    Our rescue center officially opened its doors, starting small with just 10 animals and a handful of dedicated foster homes.

  • December 2019

    Community Growth

    We successfully partnered with 5 local veterinary clinics to ensure top-notch medical care and reached over 500 successful adoptions.

  • Today

    Our Commitment Continues

    We continue to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome hundreds of animals every year. Your adoption makes our mission possible!
